Events

To recognise the World Mental Health Day, institutions, organisations, and individuals across the world are organising events as a part of the Global Mental Health Campaign, at national and international level. Considering this year’s theme - Make Mental Health and Well-being for All a Global Priority - and the inclusivity it has to offer, we encourage you to take a step, and make your contribution in spreading awareness about the cause. We hereby invite you to organise event(s) in your schools, colleges, residential complexes, or at your workplace, and directly involve yourself in the furtherance of the cause. We would love to know what you plan for the World Mental Health Day and feature your undertaking on our website.

Organized By: Jindal School of Psychology and Counselling, O.P. Jindal Global University

In Collaboration With: World Federation for Mental Health and Youth for Mental Health

As part of the WFMH Global Campaign 2022, Jindal School of Psychology and Counselling, O.P.
